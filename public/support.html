Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Tap "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in screen and enter your email. You'll receive a link to reset your password.

How do I add a movie to my watchlist?

Search for a movie or tap on any movie card, then tap the "Watchlist" button on the movie detail screen.

How do I mark a movie as watched?

On the movie detail screen, tap the "Watched" button. You can also add a rating and write a review.

Can I change my profile picture?

Yes! Go to your Profile tab, tap "Edit Profile", then tap on your profile picture to take a new photo or choose from your library.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data including your watchlist, ratings, and reviews.

Is my data private?

Yes. We take your privacy seriously. Read our Privacy Policy for full details on how we protect your information.
