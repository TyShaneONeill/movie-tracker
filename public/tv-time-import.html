How it works

1

Find your gdpr-data.zip

TV Time emailed every user a full data export before the shutdown. That zip file is your TV Time history — shows, episodes, films, ratings, and your watchlist, all in one place.

2

Drop it at pocketstubs.com or in the app

Upload the export at pocketstubs.com/tv-time-import or through the PocketStubs import screen. No manual re-entry, no retyping titles.

3

Everything gets matched in about a minute

Every show, episode, film, and watchlist item is matched against our movie and TV database automatically, so your history looks the way it did on TV Time.

What comes with you

PocketStubs is built as a full TV Time replacement, not just an import tool. When you import your TV Time history, here's what migrates:

  • Shows and episode progress
  • Films you've logged
  • Ratings
  • Your watchlist

Your history stays exportable once it's here too — no hostages. If you ever want your data out, it's yours to take.

Frequently asked questions

TV Time is closed, does my export still work?

Yes, forever. Your TV Time gdpr-data.zip export works with PocketStubs' import tool whenever you're ready — there's no deadline.

Do I need the app to import my TV Time history?

No. The importer runs right in your browser at pocketstubs.com. Apps are also available for iOS and Android if you want to keep tracking on your phone.

Is it free?

Yes. Importing your TV Time history and using PocketStubs' core tracking features is free.
